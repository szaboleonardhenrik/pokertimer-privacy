Child Safety Standards — PokerTimer - Tournament Clock

Last updated: March 10, 2026

PokerTimer - Tournament Clock is committed to the safety and protection of children. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind.

1. Our Commitment

PokerTimer - Tournament Clock (published on Google Play as "PokerTimer - Tournament Clock") strictly prohibits any content, behavior, or activity that involves, promotes, or facilitates:

  • Child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or inappropriate communication with minors
  • Sextortion or any form of exploitation targeting minors
  • Trafficking of minors
  • Any other activity that endangers the safety and well-being of children

2. About Our App

PokerTimer - Tournament Clock is a poker tournament management utility designed for adult home game organizers. The app:

  • Does not include any user-generated content features
  • Does not include chat, messaging, or social features
  • Does not collect personal information from users
  • Does not enable communication between users
  • Stores all data locally on the device only

3. Enforcement

Should any content or behavior violating these standards be identified in any context related to our app or services, we will:

  • Take immediate action to remove such content
  • Report the incident to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local law enforcement
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • Permanently ban any associated accounts or access

4. Reporting

If you become aware of any child safety concerns related to our app, please report it immediately:

  • Email: pokertimer.app@gmail.com
  • NCMEC CyberTipline: https://report.cybertip.org
