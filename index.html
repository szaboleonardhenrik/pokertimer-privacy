Privacy Policy - Poker Timer

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Poker Timer ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Poker Timer stores all user data locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Tournament settings and custom configurations
  • Saved tournament presets
  • Tournament series/league results and player standings
  • Theme and language preferences
  • Sound settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Google Play Billing. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

Subscription purchases are processed by Google Play. Google handles all payment information. We do not have access to your payment details.

3. How We Use Information

Local data is used solely to provide app functionality:

  • Remembering your tournament settings between sessions
  • Tracking tournament series results
  • Applying your preferred theme and language

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data shared with third parties is through the services described above (AdMob and RevenueCat), which is necessary for advertising and subscription functionality.

5. Data Retention

All locally stored data persists until you clear the app's data, uninstall the app, or clear your browser storage. We do not retain any data on external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Poker Timer is a tournament management utility and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: pokertimer.app@gmail.com
